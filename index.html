About

Plucked from the awe-inspiring worlds of 80s science fiction, and drenched in the taste of a cyberpunk future, Diversion is an american electro-pop band that has been able to capture an aesthetic in sound. Formed in 2013 by four childhood friends Ethan Morris, Alexander Hunt, Ellen Davis and Sophia Wallace, Diversion found their beginnings playing in clubs around San Francisco and won over crowds with their fresh, high energy sound and journey-like experience - painting a collage of electronic moods that found a massive online audience . With every successive show, their intricate sets and elaborate light and sound shows have become increasingly more complex and have come to further realize the band’s goal to create a world of experience through sound. Their first studio album “Retrofuturism” in 2016 was a smash hit, finding a place on the Billboard charts, as well as fueling the stories behind their imaginative concert experiences.
